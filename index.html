Frequently asked questions

Does Flossworks work offline?

Yes. Flossworks runs entirely on your device — no internet connection is needed to use it.

Do I need an account?

No. There's no sign-up or login — just open the app and start.

Where is my data stored?

On your device. Your images, patterns, and progress stay with you and aren't uploaded anywhere. See our Privacy Policy.

What can I make?

Turn your images into cross-stitch patterns using DMC thread colors, organize your projects, and track your stitching progress.

Can I turn a photo into a cross-stitch pattern?

Yes. Pick any photo or image and Flossworks converts it into a stitchable cross-stitch chart using real DMC thread colors.

Can I import Pattern Keeper PDFs?

Yes. You can import existing chart PDFs — including Pattern Keeper–style charts — so all your patterns live in one place.

What thread colors does Flossworks use?

Patterns use real DMC thread colors, shown with a clear symbol legend you can stitch from.

Does it work on tablets?

Yes. Flossworks is built for both Android phones and tablets.

Is Flossworks free?

Flossworks is free to download with a 30-day free trial of every feature. After the trial, a one-time purchase unlocks creating, importing, and exporting patterns — while viewing your charts and tracking your stitches stay free, forever. No subscription.

Can I bring my progress from Pattern Keeper?

Yes. Import a Pattern Keeper .pk_progress backup and Flossworks restores your stitched progress onto the matching chart, so you can pick up right where you left off.

Can I sell patterns I make?

Yes. A one-time commercial unlock lets you brand exported PDFs with your own name and logo, generate listing images, and includes a license to sell the patterns you create with the app.

Is there a subscription?

No. Flossworks is designed as a one-time purchase, not a subscription.
