Frequently asked questions

Does Flossworks work offline?

Yes. Flossworks runs entirely on your device — no internet connection is needed to use it.

Do I need an account?

No. There's no sign-up or login — just open the app and start.

Where is my data stored?

On your device. Your images, patterns, and progress stay with you and aren't uploaded anywhere. See our Privacy Policy.

What can I make?

Turn your images into cross-stitch patterns using DMC thread colors, organize your projects, and track your stitching progress.

Is there a subscription?

No. Flossworks is designed as a one-time purchase, not a subscription.
